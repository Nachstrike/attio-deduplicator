How it works

We find duplicates by matching names (fuzzy, so "Jon" = "John") and emails.

For emails: nacho@google.com and nacho@google.es are the same person — same username, same domain, just different country extension.

When we find a match:

  • Same company or no company → we merge automatically
  • Different companies → we flag it for you to decide

You get 2 CSVs:

  • Master — clean records with merged data + flagged ones marked for review
  • To delete — records to remove from Attio

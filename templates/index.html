How it works

Upload your CSV with contacts. We scan every record looking for duplicates.

Name matching: "John Smith" and "Jon Smith" → matched as duplicates (fuzzy matching catches typos and variations)

Email matching: nacho@google.com and nacho@google.es → matched as same person (we ignore the domain, only compare usernames)

When we find duplicates, we check the company field:

Same name + same company → auto-merge (we keep the most complete record and merge data from duplicates)

Same name + no company on one → auto-merge (the one without company is clearly a duplicate)

Same name + different companies → flagged for your review (could be two different people, you decide)

You get three CSV files:

1. Master CSV — clean records with merged data, upload this to Attio

2. Duplicates to delete — delete these from Attio before importing master

3. Flagged for review — same name, different company, review manually
